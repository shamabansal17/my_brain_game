📚 How to Solve
Rules
1-9 once per row, column and box
Every row, column and 3×3 box must contain 1-9 exactly once.
Elimination
Cross off impossible numbers
Look at a blank cell — any digit already in its row, column or box is eliminated. One candidate left? Fill it in.
Scanning
Find where a digit must go in a box
Pick a digit. In a 3×3 box, if only one cell can hold it, that is its home.
Naked Pairs
Two cells, same two candidates, same group
If two cells share the same two possible digits, no other cell in that row/col/box can hold either.
Hints
Green glow = easiest cell to solve next
Beginner: unlimited. Intermediate: 5. Advanced: 3. Challenging & Grandmaster: none.
